The following is a list of frequently asked questions when creating rotations.

What is displace finishes?
When entering a breakdown by assigning a particular score, the scorer has the option to specify whether the newly entered finish bumps other finishers back. By default, this is not the case. This means that two boats can get the same score for the same race.
Please note that this option is not available if the team is assigned average score as a breakdown.
What should I use for penalties?
The procedural rules specify that all penalties are scored as FLEET + 1. This is what is meant by the "Use standard scoring" option. This is the default and should be used most of the time. Note that this always results in other teams being bumped forward by one place.
In the rarest of cases, and for flexibility reasons, in keeping with the procedural rules which enable a judge to assign a score as he/she sees fit, the scorer has the opportunity to assign a particular score as part of the penalty. In such cases, the scorer also gets to choose whether to push the other finishes behind the newly assigned score, or to assign the penalty without affecting other finishes.
